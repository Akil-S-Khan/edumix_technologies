All About Edumix Technologies

Edumix Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a leading global IT solutions provider based in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. As a private limited company, we specialize in delivering innovative, customized, and scalable digital solutions to clients across the globe. Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes with cutting-edge technology that drives efficiency, growth, and competitive advantage.

Since our inception, Edumix Technologies has evolved into a trusted technology partner for startups, enterprises, and organizations worldwide. We combine deep technical expertise with strategic insight to develop robust solutions tailored to meet diverse business needs.
